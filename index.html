Welcome to Habit explorer

Introduction :

Habit Explorer is a marketing intelligence tool and is targeted for digital marketers. This tool will help the marketing teams understand and perform AI driven campaign activities.

Habitual AI is the world's first AI engine that automates the process of building the habits. This patent pending Technology enables banks and financial institutions to boost habitual usage of their digital and mobile banking platforms. It learns how customers use a digital banking product and provides personalized features recommendations that put them on the path of long-term loyalty and habitualusage.

By building customers habits, Habitual.AI delivered 145% increase in transaction, 45% reduction in customer churn and 75% increase in users who made 4 or more transaction a month. This interactive demo is design to simulate the feature recommendations that Habitual.AI is capable of generating for your unique customer base.
